Dewar's Live True

Site Build

Dewar's Live True site was my first project for the Bacardi family of products, and one of their most polished final pieces. I was brought on to help create a dynamic grid capable of displaying tiles of varying sizes and content, while always remaining evenly spaced and allocated.

We chose to work with Isotope.js because of the depth of customization available - this ended up being a good choice, as the site translates into 81 different languages depending on what country the site is viewed from. Using a general word-space test (German being the most condensed language, Korean being the most verbose) we worked to create a dynamically-resizable content area for each tile, informed by the size of its contents.

The result is a remarkably flexible grid system that still manages to look intentionally laid out - no matter what country you view it in.
