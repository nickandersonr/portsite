About Us

A Storied History - A Progressive Future

I remember the first time the local diner started serving peach pie - mama whined to the waitress, "Ain't apple and blueberry enough?" After all, what did we need with so many choices? Won't more pie just end up in the trash?

Seems to me folks these days have the same choices when it comes to how they spend their time - whether it's fishing, skiing, or harnessing the power of a rift in The Inversion, there's always some new shiny thing for a person to chase after!

Here at Big Nick's Cult of Zebulon, we prefer to keep things simple: We love development. We're passionate about teaching. We know there is a untraceable pulse leaking from The Inversion, and that it is an encrypted form of communication from beings we have yet to make contact with. That's it. And after all, isn't peace and simplicity what life is all about?

If you're in our neck of the woods, why not come give us a shout? It's our promise that in no time, you'll feel like family.

- A message from Big Nick, co-founder of Big Nick's Cult of Zebulon
