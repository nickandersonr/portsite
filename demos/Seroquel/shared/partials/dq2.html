What is your approach when treating patients
who continue to experience depressive episodes?
  • I proceed cautiously, making sure
    to give the current medication
    enough time to work
  • I consider adjusting the dose
    within the recommended dose range
  • I consider an approved adjunctive
    therapy that works differently for
    appropriate patients
  • I consider a different medication
    that is approved for the disorder,
    as patients may respond differently
